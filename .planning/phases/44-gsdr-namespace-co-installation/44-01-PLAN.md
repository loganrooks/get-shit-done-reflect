**Double-replacement safety (confirmed by deliberation):**
- `gsd-` requires `-` after `gsd`. In `gsdr-executor`, char after `gsd` is `r`, not `-`. No match.
- `get-shit-done/` requires `/` after. In `get-shit-done-reflect/`, char after `get-shit-done` is `-`, not `/`. No match.
- `/gsd:` requires `:` after. In `/gsdr:`, char after `gsd` is `r`, not `:`. No match.
- `GSD ►` is a literal 5-char string. No risk of partial match.

**What NOT to rewrite (handled by existing infrastructure):**
- `~/.gsd/knowledge/` -- Pass 1 already rewrites gsd-knowledge paths, and `~/.gsd/` has no `gsd-` pattern (it's `.gsd/`)
- `knowledge-store.md`, `kb-templates/` -- no `gsd-` prefix, unaffected
- `subagent_type="general-purpose"` -- no `gsd-` prefix, unaffected

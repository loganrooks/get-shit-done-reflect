## Decisions & Deviations

**Decisions (all under Claude's Discretion per CONTEXT.md DC-6):**

- **Argument parsing flexibility** — no rigid parser. Mode Detection describes flag parsing as instructions rather than a grammar. Follows "don't over-engineer classifier logic" prohibition.
- **Fit assessment length** — 2-4 sentences, explicitly called out in Step 2. Long enough to explain each axis choice; short enough not to preempt the auditor's own framing.
- **Number of clarifying questions** — 0-2 bounded explicitly per DC-6; in `--auto` mode, skip entirely and record ambiguity in fit assessment.
- **Session directory slug derivation** — shell snippet (tr/sed pipeline) rather than calling a gsd-tools subcommand. The `generate-slug` subcommand exists in gsd-tools but the direct shell pipeline is simpler and avoids an unnecessary process boundary for this single operation. Noted for future formalization if slug generation becomes more complex.
- **Process body prose-not-pseudocode** — per the plan's action guidance. Each step is written as instructions to a thoughtful reader, specific enough to guide execution but not a state machine.

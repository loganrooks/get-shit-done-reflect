**Selected option:** Role-split model with `about_work[]`, `detected_by`, and `written_by`.
**Rationale (auto):** PROV-01 and the audit both identify role-splitting as the core fix. Doc-only clarification preserves the mixed payload that already fails attribution. **Selected option:** Uniform required `signature` block on PLAN, SUMMARY, and VERIFICATION only.

<specifics>
## Specific Ideas

- Two-stage approach was deliberately chosen in the deliberation to avoid "trying to do philosophical design work as a quick task" — keep Stage 1 simple
- The deliberation maps GSDR skills to philosophical dialogue traditions (Socratic=explore, Dialectical=deliberate, Experimental=spike, Hermeneutic=gap, Abductive=partly reflect) — this awareness should inform how we document the skill but NOT change its implementation in Stage 1
- Open design question for 57.2: whether the discuss-phase exploration subagent should leverage explore's Socratic questioning infrastructure. This phase should not foreclose that option

</specifics>

  <action>
Create `get-shit-done/references/health-scoring.md` defining the separated scoring layer (per deliberation architecture -- LOCKED).

**Document structure:**

## 1. Overview
- Two independent dimensions: Infrastructure Health and Workflow Health
- Scores are attention guides, not decision gates (LOCKED design principle)
- Standing caveat: "Health checks measure known categories. Absence of findings does not mean absence of problems." (LOCKED)

## 2. Infrastructure Score (HEALTH-01 partial)
- Binary dimension computed from all probes with `dimension: infrastructure`
- Scoring: HEALTHY (zero FAILs, zero WARNINGs), DEGRADED (at least one WARNING, zero FAILs), UNHEALTHY (any FAIL)
- Each probe contributes independently to the infrastructure dimension

## 3. Workflow Score (HEALTH-01 partial, HEALTH-02)
- Continuous dimension computed from probes with `dimension: workflow`
- Weighted signal accumulation: critical=1.0, notable=0.3, minor=0.1 (LOCKED weights)
- Pattern deduplication: signals with identical tags counted once before weighting
- Level thresholds (configurable via health_check.workflow_thresholds):
  - LOW: weighted_sum < 2.0 (default)
  - MED: 2.0 <= weighted_sum < 5.0 (default)
  - HIGH: weighted_sum >= 5.0 (default)

## 4. Composite Traffic Light (HEALTH-03)
- 3x3 matrix mapping (infrastructure state x workflow level) to GREEN/YELLOW/RED:

```
              | Workflow LOW | Workflow MED | Workflow HIGH |
  HEALTHY     | GREEN        | YELLOW       | RED           |
  DEGRADED    | YELLOW       | YELLOW       | RED           |
  UNHEALTHY   | RED          | RED          | RED           |
```

## 5. Cache Format
- Location: `~/.claude/cache/gsd-health-score.json`
- Fields: infrastructure (HEALTHY/DEGRADED/UNHEALTHY), workflow (LOW/MED/HIGH), composite (GREEN/YELLOW/RED), weighted_sum (number), signal_count ({critical, notable, minor}), resolution_ratio (number), density_trend (increasing/stable/decreasing), checked (unix timestamp), phase (number), regime_id (string or null)
- Cache staleness: configurable via health_check.cache_staleness_hours (default 24)

## 6. Reactive Threshold (HEALTH-06)
- When cached composite score meets or exceeds reactive_threshold, session-start triggers a fresh health check
- Default reactive_threshold: RED (only trigger on RED)
- Setting to "disabled" turns off reactive triggers

The CI sensor detects failures after the fact but does not prevent completion while CI is red. Three gaps compound: (1) execute-phase has no CI gate step, (2) direct-to-main pushes bypass PR-based CI, (3) unpushed work accumulates without CI coverage.

**Counter-evidence (1 found):**

- **Impact:** Confidence maintained at high.

**Recommended action:** Remove admin bypass from branch protection. Add CI gate step to execute-phase workflow. Integrate CI sensor output as blocking check.

## What Happened

A multi-pass deliberation review chain involving two models (Claude Opus 4.6 and GPT-5.4 xhigh on Codex) completed successfully across five deliberations, five reviews, an immanent critique, an independent critical review, two targeted revision passes, and a deliberation revision pass. The chain maintained epistemic discipline throughout without degrading into either false harmony or endless revision cycles.

## Context

The review chain followed this sequence:

1. Five deliberation reviews written (Claude Opus 4.6)
2. Immanent critique applied to the review set (Claude Opus 4.6)
3. Reviews revised with corpus grounding, support/maturity labels, triangulation tables
4. Independent critical review of the revised set (Claude Opus 4.6, fresh session)
5. Cross-model assessment of independent review (GPT-5.4 xhigh)
6. Targeted review revisions: load-bearing boundary pressures, evidential unevenness marking, convergent ordering flagged
7. Deliberation revisions: governance fixes, portability scoping, genre-appropriate standing statements
8. Revision brief documenting aims and constraints

Key qualities that held:

- Boundary pressures became load-bearing rather than remaining rhetorical hedges
- Unlike objects (strongly grounded vs interpretive vs speculative reviews) were marked as different genres rather than forced into false equivalence
- The convergent roadmap ordering was flagged as potentially artifactual from shared production context
- The responsibility and community deliberations were left alone rather than being forced into premature operationalization
- The comparative-characterization deliberation's portability claims were explicitly scoped to project-local methodology
- Each pass was proportionate — targeted corrections rather than full rewrites

## Potential Cause

Several factors contributed to the chain staying disciplined:

- The immanent critique established an internal standard the reviews could be held to
- The independent critical review came from a fresh context window, providing genuine distance
- The cross-model review (GPT-5.4) provided the first truly independent assessment
- The user consistently pushed for honesty about insufficiency rather than premature closure
- The revision brief made aims and out-of-scope boundaries explicit before the deliberation revision pass
- The standing note and differentiated-readiness table gave readers a way to encounter the set's unevenness before diving into individual reviews

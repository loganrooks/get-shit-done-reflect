# Requirements Review Prompt — v1.20 GSD Reflect

You are reviewing the requirements specification for v1.20 of GSD Reflect, a self-improving agentic workflow harness. Your job is to produce a structured critique document — not a pass/fail verdict.

## What to review

Read these files in order:
1. `.planning/REQUIREMENTS.md` — the requirements to review (46 requirements, 8 categories)
2. `.planning/MILESTONE-CONTEXT.md` — the milestone steering brief with working assumptions and design constraints
3. `.planning/research/SUMMARY.md` — synthesis of 9 research documents

## Review dimensions

For each category of requirements, assess:

1. **Completeness**: Are there gaps? Requirements that the research recommends but that aren't captured? Requirements implied by the milestone goals but missing?
2. **Specificity**: Are requirements testable and atomic? Or are some vague enough that "done" is ambiguous?
3. **Traceability**: Does each requirement cite sufficient motivation? Are the citations accurate?
4. **Dependencies**: Are inter-requirement dependencies explicit? Does the ordering make sense?
5. **Feasibility**: Given the research findings, are any requirements likely to be harder than they appear? Are there hidden prerequisites?
6. **Scope appropriateness**: Should any v1.20 requirement be deferred? Should any v1.21 deferred item be pulled forward?
7. **Design constraint compliance**: Do requirements respect the 5 design constraints from MILESTONE-CONTEXT.md?
8. **Over-formalization risk**: Do any requirements risk killing productive informality? (The audit's strongest positive pattern — cross-model review — works partly because it's informal)
9. **Cross-requirement tensions**: Do any requirements contradict each other or create tension?
10. **Missing categories**: Are there capability areas the milestone should address that have no requirements at all?

## Output format

Write your review to `.planning/audits/v1.20-requirements-review.md` with this structure:

```markdown
# Requirements Review: v1.20 GSD Reflect

**Reviewer:** GPT 5.4 xhigh
**Date:** 2026-04-08
**Documents reviewed:** REQUIREMENTS.md, MILESTONE-CONTEXT.md, SUMMARY.md

## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentence overall assessment]

### [Category Name]
**Completeness:** [assessment]
**Specificity:** [assessment]
**Issues found:**
- [specific issue with specific requirement ID]

## Cross-Cutting Findings
[Findings that span multiple categories]

## Gaps Identified
[Requirements that should exist but don't]

## Recommendations
[Numbered list of specific, actionable recommendations — add/modify/remove/defer specific requirements]

## Tensions and Risks
[Requirements that create tension with each other or with design constraints]
```

Be rigorous. Be specific. Cite requirement IDs. Don't rubber-stamp.
